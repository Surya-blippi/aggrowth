FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with AG Growth Lab.

How quickly can you deliver results?

Most clients see initial improvements within 2-4 weeks. For analytics audits and attribution fixes, results are often visible immediately. Larger projects like MVP development typically take 10-30 days depending on complexity.

What types of businesses do you work with?

We primarily work with SaaS startups, DTC brands, and service businesses that are already generating revenue and looking to scale. Our ideal clients have product-market fit and want to optimize their growth engines through data-driven approaches.

Do you work with early-stage startups?

Yes, especially for MVP development and initial analytics setup. We help early-stage companies build the right foundation for growth tracking and experimentation from day one.

What's included in an analytics audit?

Our analytics audit includes a complete review of your current tracking setup, identification of data gaps, attribution analysis, conversion funnel analysis, and a detailed report with prioritized recommendations for improvement.

Can you help with ongoing optimization?

Absolutely. We offer both project-based work and ongoing optimization partnerships. Many clients start with a specific project and then work with us monthly for continued growth optimization and strategic guidance.
